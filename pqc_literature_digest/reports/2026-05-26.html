1. CoNAN: A Structure-Aware Framework for Lattice Cryptanalysis

IACR ePrint | Sat, 23 May 2026 13:22:21 +0000 | | score 23

PQC

中文摘要

CoNAN是一个将代数结构融入格安全估计的框架，通过代数诱导的同态导出低维格表示，从而复现了针对NTRU Composite、BQTRU和多变量LWE的已知攻击，并揭示非交换群环上的结构化LWE安全强度至少降低60比特。该工作为未来基于结构化格的方案提供了安全的参数选择指导。

English Summary

CoNAN is a framework that incorporates algebraic structure into lattice security estimation, exploiting algebra-induced homomorphisms to derive lower-dimensional lattice representations. It reproduces known attacks on NTRU Composite, BQTRU, and multivariate LWE, and reveals that structured LWE over noncommutative group rings can degrade security by at least 60 bits. The work guides secure parameter selection for future structured lattice-based schemes.

相关性 / Relevance

本论文直接影响了结构化格基PQC方案的安全性分析，表明代数结构可显著降低有效安全强度，对NTRU和基于LWE的提案的参数选择至关重要。

This paper directly impacts the security analysis of structured lattice-based PQC schemes by showing that algebraic structure can significantly reduce effective security, which is critical for parameter selection in NTRU and LWE-based proposals.

2. On Publicly Verifiable Tokens in Group Signatures with Message-Dependent Opening

IACR ePrint | Sat, 23 May 2026 01:34:24 +0000 | | score 19

密码学 PQC

中文摘要

本文正式定义了消息相关打开群签名（GS-MDO）中令牌的公开可验证性，明确了令牌可以在不依赖打开者私钥的情况下被公开验证其有效性的属性。建立了将令牌作为签名验证与用于打开验证之间的关系，并证明现有的基于配对和基于格的GS-MDO方案满足这一定义。此外，讨论了公开可验证令牌如何实现管理员的问责性。

English Summary

This paper formally defines public verifiability for tokens in group signatures with message-dependent opening (GS-MDO), clarifying the property that tokens can be publicly verified as valid for opening without the opener's secret key. It establishes a relationship between verifying a token as a signature and verifying its use for opening, and shows that existing pairing-based and lattice-based GS-MDO schemes satisfy this definition. Additionally, it discusses how publicly verifiable tokens enable admitter accountability.

相关性 / Relevance

本文与后量子密码相关，因为分析中包含了基于格的GS-MDO方案（Libert等2016），该方案是后量子的。公开可验证性和管理员问责性的形式化定义可指导后量子GS-MDO构造的设计。

This paper is relevant to PQC as its analysis includes a lattice-based GS-MDO scheme (Libert et al. 2016), which is post-quantum. The formalization of public verifiability and admitter accountability can guide the design of post-quantum GS-MDO constructions.

4. Ciphertext-Updatable Attribute-Based and Predicate Encryption from Lattices

IACR ePrint | Sun, 24 May 2026 08:18:18 +0000 | | score 8

密码学 theory PQC

中文摘要

我们提出了可更新密文的属性基加密（CU-ABE）和谓词加密（CU-PE），允许通过主密钥持有者生成的令牌对密文进行受控更新。我们利用基于LWE的内积函数加密提供了从ABE到CU-ABE的通用转换，并结合可锁定混淆实现CU-PE。针对有界深度电路的多跳和无限令牌扩展方案依赖于规避LWE假设。

English Summary

We introduce ciphertext-updatable attribute-based encryption (CU-ABE) and predicate encryption (CU-PE), allowing controlled ciphertext updates via tokens from the master secret key holder. We provide a generic ABE-to-CU-ABE transformation using inner-product functional encryption from LWE, and combine with lockable obfuscation for CU-PE. Extensions to multi-hop and unbounded token settings for bounded-depth circuits rely on the evasive LWE assumption.

相关性 / Relevance

高度相关；基于格假设（LWE）构建了可更新密文的ABE和PE，提供抗量子安全性。

Highly relevant; constructs ciphertext-updatable ABE and PE from lattice assumptions (LWE), offering post-quantum security.

6. Architectural Limits of Cloud TPUs in Finite-Field Cryptography

arXiv | 2026-05-25T02:46:02Z | | score 7

Hung Dang, Xuan Phu Dang, Tue Nguyen

密码学 NTT

中文摘要

本文实证评估了谷歌云TPU在有限域密码学中相比GPU的成本效率缺陷，发现因缺少宽整数ALU和空间未充分利用导致4693倍至6908倍的性能差距。作者提出了一种测量工具，将NTT操作映射为矩阵形式，实现了高K维占用率，但存在M维利用不足和Montgomery归约停顿问题。结果揭示了AI优化的脉动阵列与精确有限域算术需求之间的根本性架构不匹配，为未来密码学硬件设计提供了参考。

English Summary

This paper empirically evaluates the cost-efficiency deficit of Google Cloud TPUs compared to GPUs for finite-field cryptography, finding a 4,693x to 6,908x slowdown due to missing wide-integer ALUs and spatial underutilization. The authors introduce a measurement vehicle that maps NTT operations onto matrix forms, achieving high K-dimension occupancy but suffering from M-dimension under-utilization and Montgomery reduction stalls. The results highlight fundamental architectural mismatches between AI-optimized systolic arrays and the requirements of exact finite-field arithmetic, informing future hardware design for cryptographic workloads.

相关性 / Relevance

与后量子密码学高度相关，因为有限域算术（如用于NTT）是格基后量子方案的核心组成部分，了解TPU上的硬件瓶颈有助于为后量子密码加速实现高效实现和协同设计。

High relevance to post-quantum cryptography (PQC) because finite-field arithmetic (e.g., for NTT) is a core component of lattice-based PQC schemes, and understanding hardware bottlenecks on TPUs informs efficient implementation and co-design for PQC acceleration.
